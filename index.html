About
Each trip stores its own rate, so changing the default here does not retroactively alter history. Override the rate on any individual trip in the Log form.

IRS updates the standard mileage rate annually. Check irs.gov each January.

Fuel is not an expense category — the standard mileage rate already includes fuel, oil, maintenance, depreciation, and insurance. Valid separate deductions: parking, tolls, business-trip lodging, meals (50%), other business-purpose costs.
